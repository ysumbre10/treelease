# Tree Lease - Website Idea & Design Reference

## 1. What is Tree Lease?

**Tree Lease** is an **Agri-FinTech platform** by **AuroVil Asset Management Pvt. Ltd.** that enables individuals to **lease fruit-bearing trees** (e.g., Alphonso mango trees) as micro-assets.

**Tagline:** *"Leasing Life, Not Just Produce."*

### Core Concept
- Users **don't own the land** or **manage the farm**
- Users **own the yield** from the leased tree
- Each tree becomes a **micro-asset** that produces value over time

---

## 2. Business Model - Triple-Benefit Revenue System

### Revenue Streams per Leased Tree

| Stream | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **Home-Share (Consumption)** | A portion of the harvest (e.g., 10kg Alphonso mangoes) is delivered directly to the lessee |
| **Commercial-Share (Yield)** | Remaining harvest is monetized through Tree Lease's distribution pipeline; revenue shared with the lessee |

### Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Role | Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| Lessee (Customer) | Seasonal Owner | Earns yield/dividend + premium produce |
| Farmer | Land & Labor | Fair, stable income |
| Tree Lease | Platform & Ops | Service fee |

### 1+1 Yield Protection Model
- **1 Year**: Standard lease duration
- **+1 Year**: Built-in buffer at no extra cost
- Protects against natural variability, ensuring fair yield delivery and long-term trust

## 3. Key Platform Features (from PDF)

### Digital Asset Tracking
- Every tree has a **unique Asset ID**
- **GPS coordinates** + tree-level visibility
- Creates a **Digital Twin** of the asset

### Biological Investor Updates
- **Bloom Reports** (flowering stage)
- **Growth Tracking** (pollination to fruiting)
- **Soil & Health Updates**

### Harvest Provenance System
- Hand-picked at optimal hours (e.g., 4 AM)
- Naturally ripened (no carbide)
- Farm-to-door delivery

## 11. UI/UX Design Patterns to Follow

### Visual Identity
- **Color palette**: Greens, earth tones (browns, warm yellows), white backgrounds
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif for body; bold serif or display font for headlines
- **Imagery**: Real farm photos, orchard aerials, close-ups of fruit, farmer portraits

### Interaction Patterns
- **Card-based layouts** for browsing trees in the marketplace
- **Interactive map** (Mapbox/Google Maps) showing farm/tree GPS locations
- **Progress bars** for tree growth stages (Dormant > Bloom > Fruiting > Harvest)
- **Timeline/feed** for biological updates per tree
- **ROI/Yield calculator** - interactive slider to estimate returns
- **Sticky CTA buttons** on mobile
- **Chat widget** (WhatsApp or Intercom) for support
- **Digital certificate** - shareable image/PDF for tree ownership

### Dashboard Patterns
- **Summary cards at top** - Total invested, total earnings, active trees count
- **Line chart** - Earnings over time
- **Photo gallery** - Periodic tree/farm photos with dates
- **Activity feed** - Timeline of updates per tree (bloom, harvest, delivery, payout)

### Trust Signals
- Partner/certification logos (organic, FSSAI, etc.)
- Real farmer profiles with photos
- Transparent yield reporting
- Harvest provenance details (pick time, ripening method, delivery tracking)
